Abstract

We present a traffic monitoring tool, based on computer vision techniques, which is able to perform vehicle tracking and speed control. It uses background substraction techniques like Gaussian modelling or Stauffer & Grimson, morphological operators and stabilization algorithms to process the video, and advanced tracking methods which use Kalman filtering and mean-shift algorithms.
